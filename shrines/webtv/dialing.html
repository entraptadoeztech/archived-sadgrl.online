This page is dedicated to the lil animation/song that would play when you connect to WebTV. One user cleverly commented on a video, "They had to play really soothing music for the connecting screen so that you couldn't get angry enough to throw the damn box when it wouldn't connect, which was a lot of the time. Ahhhh.... memories."

There are actually three versions: the original, one right after WebTV got acquired by MSN but before they officially changed all of the branding (?), and one for WebTV and one for MSNTV. I personally prefer the original.
