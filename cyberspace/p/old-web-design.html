Old web design

One of my favorite things to do is look at old website layouts, especially for inspiration about future designs. The problem is, it's difficult to smoothly browse old websites, and many websites are filled with broken images preventing us from rendering it in its full glory.

Someone who is as passionate about old web design as I am shared with me some amazing scans from various print books, most of which feature full-color screenshots of old websites, images and layouts in-tact! I hope you will enjoy these as much as I do! ♥
